Rules & Regulations

1) Perfect silence should be observed in the Library.
2) Students can keep the books for two weeks at a time.
3) Books should be returned on or before the due date, which is marked on the last page of the books issued.
4)A fine of Re.1 will be imposed per day for late returning of books.
5)Books will be issued only on production of Library tickets and the Barcoded Student Identity card.
